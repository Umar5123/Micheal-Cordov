Even though you try to avoid accidents, you can be injured because another person wasn’t watching out for your safety. Whether it was a slip-and-fall, a car accident, a medical injury, a product injury, or some other type of accident, you deserve monetary compensation for the injuries and damages someone else has forced upon you.

Filing a personal injury claim or lawsuit in Phoenix is not something the average person can do on their own. There are complex laws that can affect your case, as well as difficult negotiations and paperwork you will have to complete. You must also accurately calculate your losses, investigate the accident, and collect evidence to prove negligence.

You deserve to have the help of a dedicated, meticulous legal professional who can guide you through every step of the Phoenix personal injury claim process. You should be able to focus on recovering from your injuries and getting back to work. That’s why it’s important for you to reach out to a Phoenix injury lawyer at the Law Offices of Michael Cordova.

Don’t delay in calling a lawyer at our office. Most Arizona injury cases require you to submit your claim within two years of the date of your accident. Failure to submit your claim within this time frame will prevent you and your family from receiving the compensation you’re owed.

practice Areas

  • Car Accidents

    Vehicle collisions are a leading cause of injuries and death in the Phoenix area. Fight back against a reckless or careless driver by filing a car crash claim against them.

    Learn More

  • Truck Accidents

    Commercial trucks can cause catastrophic injuries and devastating life changes for the victims involved. A trucking company, trucker, loading company, or parts manufacturer could be liable for your truck accident.

    Learn More

  • Medical Malpractice

    When a doctor caused you harm because of subpar medical treatment, they can be held responsible for harming you. Medical negligence claims can also prevent others in Phoenix from being harmed by a careless doctor.

    Learn More

  • Premises Liability

    Property owners have the responsibility of maintaining a safe property for guests and visitors. If a hazard on someone’s property went unaddressed and led to your injury, you can file a claim.

    Learn More

  • Dog Bites

    Dog owners must be held accountable when they fail to restrain their pets. When a dangerous dog has injured you, you have every right to file a claim to receive damages.

    Learn More

  • Product Injuries

    Dangerous products can cause serious injuries, and you can hold a negligent manufacturer accountable for your suffering. An attorney can investigate the product that caused you harm and do what’s possible to hold the manufacturer legally responsible.

    Learn More

  • Motorcycle Accidents

    Many people don’t watch out for motorcyclists, and you can be seriously injured as a result. Make sure a negligent driver or other responsible party doesn’t go unpunished for causing your motorcycle crash.

    Learn More

  • Wrongful Death

    Your beloved family member would still be here today if not for the careless or wrongful act of another. Receive justice for your loved one by filing a wrongful death claim.

NO FEE GUARANTEE

We offer all of our clients our “No Fee Guarantee.” Our personal injury cases are handled on a contingent fee basis. This means we do not charge attorneys fees unless we obtain compensation on your behalf.

RECOVERY PROMISE

We do not recover more than our clients. In pre-litigation cases, this policy means that if our attorneys’ fees under our agreement exceed your recovery, then we will reduce our fees. If we have to make a significant reduction from the attorneys’ fees under our contract, we will do so in order to not recover more in fees than what you will recover.
